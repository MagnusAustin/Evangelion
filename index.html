EVA PILOTS

SHINJI IKARI

Age: 14

Profession: Student

Background: Shinji is the main protagonist and the pilot of Eva Unit-01. He initially struggles with self-worth and feelings of isolation. Throughout the series, Shinji undergoes significant character development as he grapples with the responsibilities and emotional toll of being an Evangelion pilot.


REI AYANAMI

Age: 14

Profession: Student

Background: Rei is the pilot of Eva Unit-00. She is mysterious and enigmatic, with a deep connection to the Eva series. Rei's stoic demeanor and unique role in the narrative contribute to the intrigue surrounding her character.


ASUKA LANGLEY SORYU

Age: 14

Profession: Student, later UN Military Officer

Background: Asuka pilots Eva Unit-02 and is known for her brash and confident personality. With a complex background and psychological struggles, Asuka's character adds depth to the series. Her interactions with other characters and battles with Angels are central to the plot.
